When it comes to the ATO and Fair Work, "I didn't know" is not an acceptable defense. 

## Key Compliance Checkpoints

- **Tax File Number (TFN) Declaration**: Make sure all staff have completed this.
- **Superannuation Guarantee**: You must pay super to eligible employees, including some contractors.
- **Pay As You Go (PAYG)**: Understand your withholding obligations.
- **Single Touch Payroll (STP)**: Report your STP data every pay run.

Never ignore letters from regulators. Always address issues proactively.
